The Story

Every machine
has a story
worth telling.

Every design starts with a machine I can't stop thinking about — a car that rewrote the rules at Le Mans, a fighter that broke records nobody thought were breakable. I spend weeks studying reference material before a single line is placed.

The goal is always the same: capture what makes a machine feel alive. Not just the shape, but the tension, the intent, the era it defined. Each poster is designed to reward close inspection — there's always more to find the longer you look.

SK Studios is a one-person operation. Every design you see here was made by hand, from research through to final output — no shortcuts, no stock assets.

The Process
01
Research First

Before anything is designed, I spend time understanding the machine — its history, what made it significant, what it looked and felt like in its element. The details matter.

02
Designed by Hand

Every element is built from scratch. No templates, no stock photography styling. Each poster is a custom composition made to fit the subject and say something specific about it.

03
Print-Ready Quality

All designs are created at high resolution and optimized for fine-art giclee printing. What you see on screen is what you get on paper — sharp, detailed, and built to last.
